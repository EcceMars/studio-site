LOCUST

2D Mecha Pixel Art Platformer

Overview

LOCUST is a mecha themed platformer built with Godot. You pilot several mechas through hostile environments, doing mercenary work for a spatial 'mining' corporation.

The good contracts, of course, have very little to do with mining.

Art & Design

Most animations are done in Aseprite, the old way. But I have been experimenting with procedural animation, using Godot's AnimationPlayer and particle nodes.
